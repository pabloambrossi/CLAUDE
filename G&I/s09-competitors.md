# S09: What Competitors Have Built -- And What They Haven't

## VISUAL (what appears on screen)
- Split layout: **Global** (left) vs **Spain** (right)

### Global (left column)
- **Accenture:** AI Refinery ($3B+ invested), now "Reinvention Services" ($20B). Enterprise-only, >500K engagements.
- **Deloitte:** Agent Advantage (Salesforce). Function-specific agents. Big 4 overhead.
- **McKinsey/QuantumBlack:** 300+ accelerators, 25K "agents" alongside 40K humans. Fortune 500 only.
- **Omnicom-IPG:** Merged Nov 2025, $9B deal, $25B+ combined revenue. Scale play.
- **Publicis CoreAI:** EUR 300M investment, 2.3B profiles + Lotame = 91% of internet adults. Data moat.
- **WPP:** Large Marketing Model, 350+ partners. But: -8.1% revenue decline, restructuring.
- **Globant:** AI Pods (subscription model), MCP/A2A interoperability. Engineering-heavy, minimal creative/strategy.

### Spain (right column)
- **JAKALA:** "AI Factory" -- data+experience. Most direct threat but nascent proof points, lighter tech.
- **Making Science:** GMP/Adobe reseller, performance-focused. Weak in business transformation.
- **Minsait (Indra):** Broad digital transformation. No named AI products.
- **VML/The Cocktail (WPP):** "glia" engine, agents with guardrails. Constrained by WPP financial stress.

- SO WHAT: "Everyone is productizing. The globals are all >500K and enterprise-only. In Spain, no one integrates compliance + transformation for the mid-market."

- "In media, Omnicom and IPG merged in November 2025 -- $25B+ in combined revenue. WPP launched their Large Marketing Model but is dealing with an 8.1% revenue decline."
- "But here's the pattern: every single global player is above 500K per engagement, focused on Fortune 500, and has no mid-market Southern Europe play."

- "The key insight: **everyone is productizing AI services. But no one owns compliance plus transformation for mid-market Southern Europe.** That's our space."
