## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- Helvetia is the proof point that ties everything together, and it matters for three distinct reasons.
- First: this is the first time we sold a hybrid human-plus-agent team as the value differentiator to the client. Not as an internal efficiency tool — as the actual proposition. Two lanes: humans lead, communicate, decide, design, and validate. Agents analyze, propose, and execute. The result was a project that was both more profitable for us and more efficient for the client.
- Second: the proposal itself was built with AI. We gathered documentation, session transcripts, and notes. AI processed everything into structured output. Humans validated and refined. This is the new way of working — and we are already doing it.
- Third: it was genuinely cross-practice. Tech, UX, GEO, Consulting, and BX supporting. 10 deliverables, 145 assets, 10 weeks. Not a BX project with some tech support — a truly integrated engagement.
- And now the expansion. Helvetia has come back with a new request: a hiring portal redesign. And we are introducing something no one in our tier in Spain offers — synthetic user testing. AI agents running alongside real users for scalable UX research. This is the literal embodiment of Designed by Humans, Powered by AI.
- The strategic takeaway: cross-sell is working. The first cross-practice client is already expanding. This validates G&I's role in enabling growth beyond the initial engagement.
