## 2.3 The Fundamental Shift

**BEFORE:** A consultancy that DOES things for clients
**AFTER:** A consultancy that ENABLES clients to become top performers

**The 6 Enablement Layers:**

| Layer | What It Means | Why Clients Can't Do It Alone |
|-------|---------------|-------------------------------|
| 1. Data | Foundation + governance + quality + accessibility | Data is scattered, unstructured, ungoverned |
| 2. Infrastructure | Cloud, integrations, APIs, security architecture | Need enterprise-grade, not laptop with ChatGPT |
| 3. Processes | Workflows, automation, operational redesign | AI doesn't fit into old processes |
| 4. Orchestration | Agents, systems connected, MCP, A2A protocols | Making 20 agents work together safely |
| 5. Training | Capability building for internal teams | Art. 4 AI Act makes this legally mandatory |
| 6. Technology Radar | Continuous monitoring of what's new | Landscape changes weekly |

**Key argument:** AI democratization means clients WANT to internalize (and they're right to). But they CANNOT build a proprietary, scalable, secure, modular ecosystem around AI tools by themselves. Solo attempts fail 80% of the time (RAND). Partner-led implementations succeed 67% (MIT).

[Sources: 00-objective.md, 00-reality-check.md]
