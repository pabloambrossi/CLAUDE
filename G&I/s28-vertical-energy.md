## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- Energy and Utilities is our second priority vertical, and for a specific reason: AI deployed in critical infrastructure is classified as high-risk under the AI Act. That means the strictest compliance requirements apply.
- This creates a natural entry point. These clients need governance before anything else. The AI Governance Sprint maps directly to their regulatory obligation.
- Beyond compliance, the sector needs operational transformation — legacy systems in grid management, customer portals that have not been updated in a decade, sustainability reporting that is becoming mandatory.
- We have a strong existing base: 15 or more clients across Spain and Italy, AMETIC recognition in 2023, and 13 documented case studies. We have also renewed collaboration with AMETIC and IndesIA, and restarted engagement with CIONET.
- The cross-country angle is important here. Energy regulation is converging across the EU, and having presence in both Spain and Italy — the only two countries with national AI authorities — gives us a credibility advantage that competitors in a single market cannot match.
