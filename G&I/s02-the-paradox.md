  - "81%" (amber, icon: warning triangle) -- "see no bottom-line impact"

- "And only **5%** are capturing real value at scale." (BCG, 1,250 executives)
- "This is not a technology problem. It's a strategy, integration, and governance problem. And that gap -- that massive space between adoption and impact -- is exactly where G&I operates."
