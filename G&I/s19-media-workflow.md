- The Search 360 Audit is our entry product for this pillar. 15 to 30K, four to six weeks, and it covers all five search channels in one engagement.
- This is not five separate audits stitched together. It is one integrated analysis that shows how SEM, SEO, GEO, social search, and marketplace presence work together -- or fail to.

- Scrolling handles GEO execution in the short term while we build internal capability. The immediate action is hiring a GEO project manager to own the relationship and internalize the knowledge.
