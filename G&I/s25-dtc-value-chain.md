## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- This is how the three blocks connect into a single commercial engine.
- Regardless of where a client enters — Enable, Orchestrate, or Accelerate — the path follows the same logic. Entry at 25 to 100K. Implementation at 80 to 300K. Recurring managed services at 3 to 15K per month.
- The lifetime value per client is 200 to 500K euros or more. And the recurring component is what makes this sustainable — it is not project revenue that ends, it is a relationship that compounds.
- Consulting and UX/UI are transversal to every stage. They add strategic and user-centered value at each step, increasing both the quality and the stickiness of our engagements.
- This is the economic model we are building. Land with a focused entry product, expand through implementation, and retain through managed services. Classic SaaS economics applied to professional services.
