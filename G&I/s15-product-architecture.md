## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- This is the full picture. Six pillars. Two business units. One connected architecture.
- Top row: Alkemy+ pillars. These are named in the client's language. Brand & Social Commerce, Search & AI Visibility, Conversion Intelligence.
- Each pillar has a clear entry product with a defined price range, an expansion path, and a flagship offering that typically goes cross-practice.
- Bottom row: Data.Tech.AI pillars. Enable for clients who need the base. Orchestrate for governance, compliance, and design. Accelerate for real deployment and results.
- Consulting and UX/UI run transversally across all three DTC pillars.
- The key design principle: every entry product is a door into the broader architecture. A Search 360 Audit uncovers data gaps that lead to a CRO Sprint. A CRO Sprint reveals infrastructure needs that lead to Enable. This is intentional.
- Three shared principles at the bottom: hybrid teams, AI included in the price, and quarterly adaptation to market shifts.
- This is the map. Now let's go pillar by pillar, starting with Alkemy+.
