- Financial Services is our deepest vertical and the one under the most regulatory pressure right now.
- Two regulations converging: DORA for operational resilience, already in enforcement, and the AI Act for any financial model classified as high-risk. This dual pressure creates urgent demand for exactly what we offer.
- On the banking side: 75 percent plan to deploy AI agents by 2027, but 69 percent of relationship manager time is wasted on non-client tasks. Agent Launchpad maps directly to this — RM copilots that free up time for client-facing work.
- On the insurance side: the opportunity is even more open. Only 7 percent of Spanish insurers consider themselves transformative. 51 percent lack effective data governance. These clients need Enable before they can even think about agents.
- We have proof points across both sub-sectors: BBVA, MAPFRE, Helvetia, AXA, Unicaja, Bankinter. This is not a theoretical play — we are already in the room.
- Legacy modernization in core banking alone represents massive ticket sizes. ThoughtWorks estimates 57 billion in lost revenue for banks that do not modernize.
