## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- This is Alkemy Spain as the market knows us today. Two business units, strong teams, real expertise.
- Alkemy+ covers brand experience, media, and martech. Data.Tech.AI covers technology, data, AI, UX, and consulting under Letizia.
- The talent is here. The capabilities are real. But look at how it is organized -- by internal discipline.
- When a client asks "how do I sell on TikTok?", that answer lives across BX, Media, and Martech. Today they have to assemble it themselves.
- This is not a criticism. It is how most agencies are built. But the market is moving away from buying hours by discipline.
