  - Brand presence audit across social platforms

  - Channel strategy definition

  **Stage 3: PRODUCTION (Week 5-6)**

  **Stage 4: LAUNCH & OPTIMIZE (Week 7-8)**
  - Phased activation across platforms
  - Performance monitoring setup
  - First optimization cycle
  - Results report + expansion recommendations

- **Cross-sell triggers** (callout boxes below the flow):
  - Data gaps discovered at Audit --> CRO Sprint (Conversion Intelligence)
  - Search visibility gaps --> Search 360 Audit (Search & AI Visibility)
  - Infrastructure needs --> Legacy Blueprint (Enable)
  - Compliance questions --> AI Governance Sprint (Orchestrate)

- So What: "8 weeks from kickoff to live results. And every engagement naturally opens doors to adjacent pillars."

## VERBAL (what the presenter says)
- This is the Social Storefront in practice. Eight weeks, four stages, one gate.
- The gate after Blueprint is critical. We do not start producing until the client has signed off on strategy. This protects scope and builds trust.
- Production uses our BX team augmented with Cosmic for video and AI tools for high-volume asset creation.
- But the real value of showing this workflow is what happens at the edges. Every audit uncovers needs beyond social. Data gaps lead to CRO. Search gaps lead to our Search 360 Audit. Infrastructure gaps lead to the DTC Enable pillar.
- These cross-sell triggers are not forced. They emerge naturally from the work. That is why the architecture is designed the way it is.
